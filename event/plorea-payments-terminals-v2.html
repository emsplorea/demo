Combined status indicator PLBR-1227
One indicator per row. Priority: Purple > Red > Yellow > Green
Merchant failed
Highest priority. Adyen merchant assignment failed — payments may route to the wrong account. Click to retry.
Offline
Terminal has no contact with the system. Check network or power.
Pending / fault
Either online with a non-critical fault, or Adyen reassign is waiting for the next sync window (~3h).
Healthy
Online, healthy, and correctly assigned to the right merchant account.
